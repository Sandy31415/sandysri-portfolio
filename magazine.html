Magazine Project

My evolution as a designer

Challenge: Create a magazine chronicling your experience in first year in the GBDA program.
Tools used: Adobe Indesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
Timeframe: April 2025

This magazine was created as part of a school project and features original photography captured throughout the city of Waterloo. It documents my experience as a first-year student in the GBDA program, exploring themes of exploration, growth, and hope for the future.
The design incorporates bubbly, rounded typography and bright shades of blue and purple to reflect the optimism I felt toward the end of the year. I chose a less traditional layout approach, weaving images around and between blocks of text, to reinforce the idea of exploration and winding, unexpected paths.
