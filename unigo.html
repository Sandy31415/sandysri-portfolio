UniGo: A Campus Navigation App

1 / 10

Challenge: Create an app to solve a pervasive problem faced by undergraduate university students. 
Tools used: Figma
Timeframe: October 2025 - December 2025
Personal roles: Research, Persona creation, Prototyping, User Testing

The app UniGo was created in response to a challenge that many undergraduate university students face: navigating their campus.  As a group, we conducted user research through extensive surveys to uncover patterns in navigation. 92% of participants reported relying solely on navigation apps such as Google Maps and Apple Maps; the majority of navigation issues occurred indoors. As such, we decided to create an app that was tailored to students’ schedules and offered both GPS style and AR style navigation within buildings, tunnels, and bridges

Personas

Through user interviews conducted with several students accross the University of Waterloo, I developed three personas to reflect common pain points, habits, and needs of undergraduate university students looking to navigate their campus.
