UniGo: A Campus Navigation App

1 / 7

Navigation on university campuses is an often-overlooked challenge. A 2025 national survey found that 90% of first-year students have experienced getting lost on campus, a trend echoed in our user research at the University of Waterloo. Students reported feeling disoriented when navigating complex tunnel and bridge systems, winding hallways, and unofficial shortcuts—issues that existing tools like Google Maps and Apple Maps fail to address. To solve this, our team created UniGo, an app tailored to campus navigation. With indoor navigation, departure-time prompts, standard and AR navigation modes, and robust accessibility customization, UniGo empowers students to reach their destinations confidently and on time.
