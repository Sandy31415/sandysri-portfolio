Sunbot: A Health Management Robot

Challenge: Create a solution that addresses the gaps in chronic care faced by residents in Waterloo managing chronic illnesses.
Tools used: Figma, Blender
Timeframe: October 2025
Personal roles: Research, ideation, prototyping screen interface.

Persona and Empathy Map

The Screen Interface

Our Final Solution

Sunbot is a chronic health management robot. It comes equipped with a smart pill dispenser that lights up to indicate which medication should be taken, alongside accompanying information on the screen. With its tracking features and notification system, it helps users to stay on track with their lifestyle management and check ins with medical professionals. Its tactile, button-focused interface makes it more comfortable for elderly users than apps or websites; its slightly anthropomorphized appearance also creates a sense of familiarity for users.
