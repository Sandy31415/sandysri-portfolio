Lumr: Your Environmental Buddy

Challenge: How might we use design and technology to make climate awareness and sustainable action easier to understand and practice in everyday life?

Personal roles: Research, ideation, prototyping one user flow.

Lumr is a web browser extension that tracks the contents of a user's shopping cart. It assigns points and ratings to the user's cart based on the following four factors:

  • carbon emissions in production
  • Water usage in production
  • average rate of repurchase (for clothing items)
  • average shelf life (for food items)

Lumr highlights items that bring the user's score down, and offers alternatives both within the brand and externally. It provides the user with links for a seamless shopping experience.

Once the user has made a purchase, Lumr assigns a certain number of points to the user's account. These points represent the long term savings the user has made, both by purchasing products that will last longer and be used to completion, and by investing in the long term future of the planet. These points can be accumulated and converted into monetary rewards.
